The Golden Grain of Asgard

Description

From the hallowed pages of The Allfather’s Recipes

To honor the Wanderer at your table, one must craft the Sun-Thread Bread, known to the common folk as egg pasta. It is a craft as old as the world-tree, yet swifter than Sleipnir’s gallop once you master the art. Many fear the labor, but the Allfather knows that greatness requires but a moment of true focus.
