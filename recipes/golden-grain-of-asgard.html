The Golden Grain of Asgard

Description

From the hallowed pages of The Allfather’s Recipes

To honor the Wanderer at your table, one must craft the Sun-Thread Bread, known to the common folk as egg pasta. It is a craft as old as the world-tree, yet swifter than Sleipnir’s gallop once you master the art. Many fear the labor, but the Allfather knows that greatness requires but a moment of true focus.

The Sacrifices Required

  • Flour
  • Eggs

The Ritual of Creation

  1. Heap your flour. Use your fist to carve a deep well in the center, like the crater left by Mjolnir.
  2. Crack your eggs into the hollow. Take a fork—or your fingers, if you possess the courage—and whisk the eggs gently, being careful not to breach the floury walls too soon.
  3. Slowly, pull the inner walls of the flour into the pool. Mix until a shaggy, chaotic mass forms. This is the primordial state of Ginnungagap.
  4. Now, use your hands. Knead the dough with the fury of a storm.
  5. The dough must rest to find its peace.
  6. Roll the dough thin until you can see the grain of the wood beneath it, then slice it into ribbons or shapes of your choosing.

The final ascendance

Drop the pasta into a cauldron of water, salted like the Great Ocean. Boil it for a mere 2 to 4 minutes. When it rises to the surface, it has ascended. Serve it with butter, herbs, or the spoils of your hunt.
